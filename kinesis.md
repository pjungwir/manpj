multimedia keys:

    F3: Previous track
    F4: Play/pause
    F5: Next track
    F9: Mute
    F10: Volume down
    F11: Volume up

    turn off: hold equal (=) and press p (for PC).
    turn off: hold equal (=) and press w (for windows).
    turn off: hold equal (=) and press m (for Mac).
    turn on:  hold equal (=) and press n.

to see what your keys are sending:

    acpi_listen
